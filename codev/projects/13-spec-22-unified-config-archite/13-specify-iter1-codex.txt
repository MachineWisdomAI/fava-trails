### [MEDIUM — FIXED] server.py:164 — GlobalConfig.__dict__ not Pydantic V2 safe
GlobalConfig.__dict__ in Pydantic V2 does not recursively serialize nested models.
With hooks: list[HookEntry] now present, hook plugins receiving this as the startup
config dict would get raw HookEntry instances instead of plain dicts.

Fix applied: model_dump(mode='json') for full recursive JSON-compatible serialization.
